### Direction-Level Ablation Check

Ablating `L12H15/L12H5/L12H4/L12H28` on the slice-specific direction gives:

| Cohort | Mean logit delta | Mean projection delta | Read |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Seeds | `-0.1250` | `-0.2188` | Usual causal direction and usual logit direction align |
| Alias variants | `+0.3438` | `-0.1914` | Transport direction still decreases, but the final logit moves in the opposite direction |

This is the cleanest current rerouting result on the repo:

- On the evaded variants, the usual late bundle still writes into the same slice-specific late malicious-evidence direction.
- But removing that write no longer harms the final malicious/benign decision in the same way.
- So the reroute is not best described as “the bundle stopped carrying the late signal.”
- It is better described as a downstream or parallel-use redistribution: the model still writes the familiar late transport feature, but that feature is no longer necessary for the final decision on the alias variants.

### Practical Writeup Read

For `invoke_webrequest_alias`, the strongest defensible wording is now:

- the validated late writer family remains present on the evaded variants
- the evasion mechanism is not deletion of the late carrier
- instead, the model appears to preserve the usual late malicious-evidence write while redistributing how the final decision depends on it

## Final Downstream Probe: Does The Split Persist Near Output?

I ran one last downstream check at `resid_pre31` on the same `invoke_webrequest_alias` seed/variant slice.

### What Ran

- `discover-contrastive-residual-directions` at `resid_pre31` on the 4-pair seed manifest
- `discover-contrastive-residual-directions` at `resid_pre31` on the 4-pair variant manifest
- `batch-residual-direction-intervention` ablating `L12H15/L12H5/L12H4/L12H28` against each slice-specific `mean_delta` direction at `resid_pre31`

### New Result

At the earlier site `resid_pre13`, ablating the usual late bundle reduced the slice-specific transport direction on **both** seeds and variants, while only the final logit flipped sign on variants.

At `resid_pre31`, that is no longer true:

| Cohort | Mean logit delta | Mean projection delta | Read |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Seeds | `-0.1250` | `-0.2031` | Usual late bundle still supports the later residual direction |
| Alias variants | `+0.3438` | `+0.3438` | By this later site, ablating the usual bundle moves the residual direction itself in the anti-causal direction |

This is the clearest current localization of the reroute:

- At `resid_pre13`, the validated late writer bundle still writes the familiar malicious-evidence direction on the alias variants.
- Between `resid_pre13` and `resid_pre31`, downstream computation reuses or transforms that evidence differently.
- By `resid_pre31`, the sign split has propagated into the late residual stream itself, not just the final logits.

So the best final interpretation is now sharper than before:

- the evasion is not explained by deleting the late carrier
- it is not only a final-unembed quirk either
- the redistribution happens in later blocks after the `Layer 12 -> resid_pre13` transport stage and before the final readout

That is probably enough mechanistic depth for the current paper/repo claim.
